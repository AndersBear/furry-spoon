Keeping the Faith




Delusions about an afterlife,
pushing yourself beyond belief,
how it will be,
who you will see,
maybe for an eternity?
The potential for a pleasant rebirth
might seem to have much more worth,
forever is too long of a time,
pointless on the number line,
if human, who will you be?
Will you remember the "I" that was "me"?
residual artifacts left behind,
spark new ideas in your mind,
the notion of
bilocation,
in time and space and history,
reincarnation
is a keen mystery,
a super cool trick,
seeing yourself
in a TV flick,
but you can't influence,
no comment, no touch, not even a hug,
the devil knows who he will be:
a bug,
four millions of spirits,
they know no-no diff,
It's better than living
as a stiff!




You might say there's only a fine line
between cynicism and buddhism,
but once you see the difference,
you realize that one goes to the extreme
while the other just sits there.
